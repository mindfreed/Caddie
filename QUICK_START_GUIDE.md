# Freedmind AI Caddy: Quick-Start Guide
"How to put the Alliance Scout in your pocket."

1. **The Activation**: Open the link provided in your purchase email using Safari (iOS) or Chrome (Android).
2. **The Install**: Tap the 'Share' icon (iOS) or the three dots (Android) and select "Add to Home Screen." The Caddy mascot icon will now appear on your phone like a native app.
3. **The First Scout**: Go to your favorite offer wall (Freecash, KashKick, etc.), copy the title of a game, and open the Caddy.
4. **Monitor the Magic**: Watch the Magic Numbers update live. The Caddy will immediately calculate your True Hourly Rate, flag any False Summits, and start tracking your Total Time Refunded.
